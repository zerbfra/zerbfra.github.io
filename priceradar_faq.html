Why PriceRadar

Amazon's products are often subjected to price variations, either up or down: for this reason, you can select for every product a target price and get a push notification when the price drops!

How it works?

When our systems identify a price lower than the price you desire for a product, they send a notification to your device, so you can istantly purchase the item and save money.

Do I need an Amazon account?

It's not necessary to have an Amazon account, you can track the items that you like anyway. The app works on all items available on Amazon's worldwide stores.

Wish List importer

Find Amazon Wish List link

To find the correct wish list url, open the official Amazon app and from the menu select "Your Wish List".

Then tap on the "List Action" button on the top right and select "Share". Select "Copy" from the share menu and paste it in the PriceRadar wishlist field.

I can't import my wish list

Ensure that you've copied the correct wish list link from the official Amazon app or from the website. Make sure that your wishlist is set to public.

If you still have troubles, please contact the developer from the app with details about your wish list.
